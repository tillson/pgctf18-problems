PicoCTF Competition

PicoCTF is one of the first CTFs. Their competitoins brought new problems no one's ever seen before, creating a really good resource for students to study from and prepare for our upcoming competition.

PicoCTF.com writeup
EasyCTF Competition

EasyCTF is a great place for people who have never tried out a CTF before. The problems range from very easy to very hard. This competition was aimed at high-school students. the actual competition has been take down but definately take a look at the writeup.

HSCTF High School CTF
